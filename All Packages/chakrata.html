About the Destination

Why Chakrata Should Be Your Next Weekend Escape

Chakrata is a quiet cantonment town in Uttarakhand's Dehradun district, perched at an altitude of 7,000 feet in the Jaunsar hills. Unlike Mussoorie or Nainital, this destination has remained beautifully untouched — no crowds, no commercialisation, just thick deodar forests, valley views, and the kind of silence that only mountains can offer. For travellers searching for cheap hotels in Chakrata, budget stays, or even premium cottages and resorts in Chakrata, the destination offers a wide range of accommodation across every budget.

The Chakrata tour package from Delhi is one of our most popular offerings at Kafall Travels. At just 335 km from Delhi, Chakrata makes for a perfect 2-night, 3-day weekend trip — you leave Friday evening and return Sunday night feeling genuinely refreshed. Whether you're looking for a chakrata honeymoon package, a family holiday, or a solo nature retreat, this hill town delivers every time.

The region is best known for the spectacular Tiger Falls — one of India's tallest direct waterfalls at 312 feet. Beyond that, Chakrata offers Deoban for panoramic Himalayan views, Mundali for snow, and Chilmiri Neck for some of the finest sunsets in Uttarakhand. The dense forests around the town are home to leopards, Himalayan black bears, and an extraordinary variety of birds — making it a strong choice for nature photography and trekking enthusiasts.

Sightseeing

Places to Visit in Chakrata

Chakrata tourist places span waterfalls, snow meadows, dense forests, ancient temples, and sky-touching viewpoints — here are the must-sees on every chakrata itinerary.

Tiger Falls
At 312 feet, one of India's tallest direct waterfalls. A short forest trek leads to the base — powerful and completely unspoiled. Stay nearby at hotels near Tiger Falls Chakrata for early morning access.
Deoban
A dense deodar forest plateau at 9,000 ft with unobstructed views of the Himalayan snow range. One of Chakrata's finest spots for sunrise photography and nature walks.
Mundali
The snow destination near Chakrata. From December to March, Mundali receives heavy snowfall and is perfect for playing in the snow without the crowds of bigger hill stations.
Chilmiri Neck
A popular viewpoint near the market offering panoramic views of the valley and distant snow peaks, especially spectacular during sunset hours.
Kanasar
An ancient deodar and oak forest with an old cricket ground and a serene campsite atmosphere. Great for picnics, forest walks, and birdwatching.
Mahasu Devta Temple
An ancient temple of local deity Mahasu, deeply significant to the Jaunsar-Bawar community. A culturally rich stop on your chakrata sightseeing package.
