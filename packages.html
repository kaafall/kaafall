Popular Uttarakhand Destinations, Explained

For a spiritual journey, the Kedarnath Yatra package and Badrinath Yatra package cover two of the four Char Dham shrines, while Jageshwar offers a quieter, less-crowded temple trip through ancient deodar groves. Travellers chasing snow usually choose the Auli tour package for its cable-car views of Nanda Devi, or the winter meadows covered in the Chakrata weekend trip.

Short on time? Our Chopta package, Kanatal package and Mukteshwar package are built for a quick 2-3 day escape from Delhi without needing extra leave. Families and couples often prefer the classic hill stations — the Mussoorie tour package, Nainital tour package and Lansdowne tour package — all known for comfortable stays and easy sightseeing. For wildlife lovers, the Jim Corbett safari package covers India's oldest national park, and for river life and yoga, the Rishikesh tour package stays right along the Ganga ghats. If it's offbeat you're after, the Munsyari package and Tehri Lake package stay far from the usual crowds while still delivering big Himalayan views.
