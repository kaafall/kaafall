Privacy & Policy

Effective March 16, 2026  ·  Your privacy matters to us.

01 — Information We Collect

What Information We Collect

When you reach out to Kaafall Travels — whether it's for a Char Dham yatra, an Ek Dham trip, or any other destination across Uttarakhand — we collect the basic details you share with us. This includes your name, phone number, email address, and travel dates, submitted through our booking inquiry or contact form. We also collect some technical data automatically when you browse our site, like your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. That's purely to keep the website running well and improve your experience over time.

02 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

Everything we collect has one purpose — helping you plan and book your trip. We use your details to process inquiries, confirm bookings, send trip-related updates, and sort out any issues along the way. Occasionally we may send you information about new packages or seasonal offers from Uttarakhand — you can opt out of those anytime, no questions asked. We do not sell your data. We do not trade it. Simple as that.

03 — Data Sharing

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We only share your information with our team, and only what's actually needed to complete your booking. We also work with a few trusted tools to keep the website running — things like payment processing and basic analytics — and they handle your data strictly for those purposes, nothing more. If there's ever a legal requirement from a court or government authority, we may be obligated to share certain information. Outside of that, your data stays with us.

04 — Your Rights

Your Rights & Data Security

You can view, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time — just drop us an email at uk.kafall@gmail.com and we'll take care of it. Our systems use HTTPS encryption and secure data storage to keep your information protected. No system on the internet is completely immune to risk, but we take every reasonable step to make sure yours is safe. This policy may be updated from time to time — continuing to use our platform means you're okay with the current version.

Questions about your privacy?

Reach out to us — we're happy to help you understand how your data is used.
