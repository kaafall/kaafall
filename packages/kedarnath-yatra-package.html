Day-by-Day Plan

5N 6D Kedarnath Itinerary

Your Kedarnath Yatra begins the moment we pick you up from Delhi. Over 6 days you'll travel through the holy town of Haridwar, wind along the Mandakini river valley through Guptkashi, reach the last motorable point at Sonprayag, and then trek, ride, or fly to the sacred Kedarnath Temple — one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, nestled at 11,755 ft against the Kedarnath and Bharatkund peaks. Every logistic is handled — you focus only on the darshan.
1

Delhi → Haridwar

Departure · Ganga Aarti Evening

  • Morning pickup from Delhi (5–6 AM)
  • Drive via Meerut & Roorkee to Haridwar
  • Hotel check-in by afternoon
  • Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri
  • Dinner and overnight stay at Haridwar
2

Haridwar → Guptkashi

Devprayag · Rudraprayag Sangam

  • Early breakfast; drive via Devprayag (Sangam)
  • Photo stop at Rudraprayag — Alaknanda & Mandakini confluence
  • Continue along the Mandakini valley
  • Arrive Guptkashi by evening; check-in
  • Visit Vishwanath Temple, Guptkashi
  • Dinner and rest for the yatra ahead
3

Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund → Kedarnath

Trek / Pony / Helicopter

  • Early breakfast; drive to Sonprayag & Gaurikund
  • Begin the 16 km trek, pony ride, or helicopter transfer
  • Pass Rambara, Linchauli & Bhimbali checkpoints
  • Arrive Kedarnath by evening; check-in at guesthouse
  • First view of the temple against snow peaks
  • Evening aarti at the temple courtyard
4

Kedarnath Temple Darshan

Main Darshan Day · Bhairav Nath Temple

  • Early morning queue for VIP/general darshan
  • Main darshan at Kedarnath Jyotirlinga
  • Visit Bhairav Nath Temple & Adi Shankaracharya Samadhi
  • Optional short walk to Vasuki Tal (weather permitting)
  • Begin descent to Gaurikund by afternoon
  • Overnight at Sonprayag / Guptkashi
5

Guptkashi → Rudraprayag → Rishikesh

Return Journey · Rishikesh Evening

  • Breakfast & check-out; drive towards Rishikesh
  • Optional stop at Devprayag Sangam
  • Evening at Ram Jhula / Laxman Jhula, Rishikesh
  • Optional Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat
  • Dinner and overnight stay at Rishikesh
6

Rishikesh → Delhi Departure

Check-out · Drive Home

  • Breakfast & hotel check-out
  • Drive back to Delhi via Haridwar & Meerut
  • Drop back at Delhi by evening (6–8 PM)
  • Yatra complete — blessings carried home 🙏

Frequently Asked Questions

Kedarnath Temple is located in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand at an altitude of about 3,584 meters (11,755 ft). It is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and part of the Char Dham and Chota Char Dham pilgrimage circuits.
Kedarnath is approximately 460 km from Delhi to Sonprayag by road, which takes 12–14 hours. From Gaurikund, pilgrims then trek, take a pony, palki, or helicopter for the remaining 16 km to the temple.
By road: Delhi → Haridwar → Rudraprayag → Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund, then a 16 km trek, pony, palki or helicopter ride to Kedarnath. Kafall arranges the full road journey and can also book helicopter tickets on request.
The Kedarnath Temple typically opens in late April or early May (Akshaya Tritiya) and closes around late October or early November (Bhai Dooj) due to heavy snowfall in winter.
The 16 km trek from Gaurikund to Kedarnath is moderately challenging due to altitude and steep sections. Pony, palki, and helicopter options are available for those who cannot trek, including senior citizens.
Yes, helicopter services operate from Sersi, Phata and Guptkashi to Kedarnath. Kafall's premium package includes helicopter booking assistance, though seats are limited and subject to weather and availability.
Yes, with the pony, palki (doli) or helicopter options, elderly and less mobile pilgrims can comfortably complete the yatra. We recommend the Standard or Premium plan for senior citizens.
Warm woollens, a good pair of trekking shoes, rain gear, a walking stick, personal medication, and identity proof. Weather can change quickly even in summer months.
Yes, many pilgrims combine Kedarnath with Badrinath as part of the Char Dham Yatra. Kafall offers combined Kedarnath-Badrinath packages on request.
Private cab from Delhi, verified hotel stays en route, breakfast and dinner, and a dedicated support team throughout. Helicopter tickets and pony/palki charges are optional add-ons depending on the plan selected.
