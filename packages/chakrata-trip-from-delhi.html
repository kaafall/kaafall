Day-by-Day Plan

3N 4D Chakrata Itinerary

Your Chakrata journey starts the moment we pick you up from Delhi. Over 4 days you'll wind through Dehradun valleys, stand under one of India's tallest waterfalls, trek through ancient deodar forests, catch a snow meadow at Mundali, and watch the Himalayas glow at sunrise from Deoban. We've planned every detail — all you need to do is show up.
1

Delhi → Dehradun → Chakrata

Departure · Overnight Drive · Arrival

  • Evening pickup from Delhi (8–10 PM)
  • Overnight drive via Dehradun & Vikasnagar
  • Breakfast stop en route
  • Arrive Chakrata by morning (7–9 AM)
  • Hotel check-in, freshen up & rest
  • Evening walk to Chilmiri Neck viewpoint
2

Tiger Falls + Kanasar Forest

Waterfall Trek · Dense Deodar Forest

  • Breakfast at hotel; start by 9 AM
  • Drive to Tiger Falls — 312 ft waterfall trek
  • Splash & photography at the base
  • Lunch break at local dhaba
  • Kanasar forest walk — ancient oak & deodar
  • Bonfire & dinner at camp
3

Deoban + Mundali Snow Point

Himalayan Sunrise · Snow Meadows

  • Early start (6 AM) — drive to Deoban (9,000 ft)
  • Sunrise photography with Himalayan views
  • Nature walk through Deoban plateau forests
  • Drive to Mundali — snow point
  • Snow play or meadow picnic by season
  • Return to Chakrata; local market stroll
4

Chakrata → Delhi Departure

Check-out · Scenic Drive · Home

  • Early breakfast & hotel check-out
  • Quick stop at Ram Tal garden
  • Drive back through Dehradun
  • Optional Dehradun bakery stop
  • Drop back at Delhi by evening (6–8 PM)
  • Trip memories packed, hearts full 🏔️

Frequently Asked Questions

Chakrata is a serene hill station in Uttarakhand, located at an altitude of around 2,118 meters (7,000 ft) in the Jaunsar-Bawar hills of Dehradun district. It's known for its dense deodar forests, powerful waterfalls, and unobstructed Himalayan views.
Chakrata is approximately 335 km from Delhi by road. The journey takes around 7–9 hours. Most guests prefer a Friday night departure to reach Chakrata by Saturday morning.
By road: Delhi → Dehradun → Vikasnagar → Chakrata. Kafall arranges private cabs for a comfortable door-to-door journey. By train: Take a train to Dehradun and then a cab to Chakrata (~100 km).
Absolutely! A 3N/4D trip gives you enough time to cover Tiger Falls, Deoban, Mundali, and Chilmiri without feeling rushed. Many travellers leave Friday night and return Monday evening.
Chakrata is most famous for Tiger Falls — one of India's tallest direct waterfalls at 312 feet. Also known for Deoban's panoramic Himalayan sunrise views, Mundali snow point, the ancient Jaunsar tribal culture, and rare tranquility.
Yes! Chakrata offers cottages and resorts perfect for honeymoon couples, and its peaceful, safe environment is great for families with children. We have dedicated couple and family packages.
Trekking through deodar forests, waterfall visits, birdwatching, nature photography, bonfire evenings, snowfall at Mundali (winter), sunrise viewpoints at Deoban, and exploring Jaunsar-Bawar culture.
Yes! From December to February, Mundali receives heavy snowfall. Deoban and higher areas also see snow. A winter Chakrata trip is immersive and far less crowded than typical snow destinations.
Yes — from budget guesthouses (₹1,500/night) to premium mountain-view cottages and resorts (₹4,000+/night). Kafall partners only with verified, quality-checked properties.
