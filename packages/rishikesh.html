Brief Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi Departure

Evening departure from Delhi. Overnight journey to Rishikesh via comfortable bus. Snacks provided on board.

Day 02

Rishikesh Arrival & Adventure

Morning arrival, freshen up at camp. 16 km White Water Rafting on Ganga. Evening Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. Bonfire night at camp.

Day 03

Sightseeing & Explore

Yoga session (Comfort Plan). Visit Lakshman Jhula, Ram Jhula, Beatles Ashram & local markets. Evening departure for Delhi.

Day 04

Delhi Arrival

Early morning arrival back in Delhi with beautiful memories of Rishikesh.
