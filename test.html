Char Dham Yatra Package

The Char Dham Yatra takes you through four of the holiest shrines in the Himalayas — Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath — each set against dramatic mountain backdrops and centuries of devotion. If time is limited, a 2 Dham or single-shrine yatra covers the journey that matters most to you. Every itinerary is planned with realistic travel days, verified hotel stays, and local support at each stop, so you can focus on the darshan and the mountains, while we take care of the logistics along the way.
